Why Detroit Patients Choose Our Delivery Service

For many patients — especially seniors, those managing chronic conditions, or anyone with limited mobility — getting to the pharmacy every month is a real challenge. Fenkell Rx Pharmacy was founded on the belief that every Detroit resident deserves convenient, affordable access to their medications.

Our free home delivery service means you never have to choose between your health and your schedule.

How Our Free Delivery Works

  • Step 1 — Set up your profile: Call us at (313) 519-5700 or transfer your prescription online. We'll get your insurance and delivery address on file.
  • Step 2 — We fill your prescription: Our pharmacists verify and fill your order accurately, with a full pharmacist review before it leaves our hands.
  • Step 3 — We deliver to your door: Your medications arrive in a sealed, discreet package. For controlled substances or refrigerated medications, a signature may be required.

Great for Chronic Care Patients

If you take multiple medications every month, ask us about our Medication Synchronization program. We align all your refills to one single delivery date per month — so everything arrives together, on schedule, with no phone calls required.

We also offer Blister Packaging (pre-sorted daily dose packs) to make managing multiple medications simple and safe. Perfect for seniors or anyone managing a complex medication regimen.

Switching Your Pharmacy? We Handle It All.

Transferring your prescriptions to Fenkell Rx Pharmacy is completely free and takes just a few minutes. Call us with your current pharmacy's name and phone number and we'll coordinate the transfer directly — you don't have to do a thing.
