Compounding Pharmacy in Detroit, MI

When commercially available medications don't fit your needs, Fenkell Rx Pharmacy creates a custom solution — precise, safe, and made specifically for you.

What Is Compounding?

Pharmaceutical compounding is the preparation of a customized medication tailored to an individual patient's specific needs. Our licensed compounding pharmacists at Fenkell Rx Pharmacy work directly with you and your physician to formulate medications that are simply not available off the shelf.

Whether you need a different strength, a different form, or a formulation free of allergens and dyes, we have the expertise to deliver exactly what your body requires.

Our Detroit Compounding Services

  • Pediatric Flavor Adjustments — We reformulate children's medications in appealing flavors like cherry, grape, bubblegum, and watermelon to make daily dosing easier for parents and kids alike.
  • Allergen-Free Formulations — We remove inactive ingredients such as dyes, preservatives, gluten, lactose, and sugar that may cause sensitivities or allergic reactions.
  • Veterinary Compounding — Custom medications for pets including dogs, cats, horses, and exotic animals in treats, flavored liquids, or transdermal gels for easy administration.
  • Alternative Dosage Forms — Tablets reformulated as topical creams, suppositories, lozenges, or nasal sprays for patients who cannot swallow standard oral medications.
  • Discontinued Medications — If your medication has been discontinued by its manufacturer, we can often recreate it to your prescribed specifications.
  • Bio-identical Hormone Therapy (BHRT) — Customized hormone preparations dosed to your physician's exact specifications.
