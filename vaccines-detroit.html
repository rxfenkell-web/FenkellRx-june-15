Flu Shot (Influenza)

Annual influenza vaccine for all ages. The single most important thing you can do each fall to protect yourself and the people around you.

All ages · Annual

COVID-19 Booster

Updated formula targeting current strains. Recommended yearly alongside the flu shot for most adults and all high-risk individuals.

All ages · Updated formula

Shingrix (Shingles)

Two-dose series for adults 50 and older. Shingrix is over 90% effective — far stronger than the older Zostavax. Covered by Medicare Part D.

Ages 50+ · 2-dose series

RSV Vaccine

Single-dose protection against respiratory syncytial virus for adults 60 and older. Covered by Medicare Part B.

Ages 60+ · Single dose

Pneumococcal (Pneumonia)

Protects against bacterial pneumonia. Recommended for adults 65 and older and for younger adults with certain health conditions.

Ages 65+ or high-risk

Tdap / Td

Tetanus, diphtheria, and pertussis (whooping cough). Adults need a Td booster every 10 years; Tdap once as an adult booster.

All ages · Booster every 10 yrs

Hepatitis A & B

Recommended for travel, occupational exposure, or personal protection. Available individually or as a combination series.

Travel · Occupational
