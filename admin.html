🔍 SEO & Search Engine Settings

Google Search Console Verification

1. Go to search.google.com/search-console and add your property.
2. Choose "HTML tag" as the verification method.
3. Copy only the code value from the meta tag Google gives you (the part after content=").
4. Paste it in the field below and hit Save All Content.
Google will automatically find the tag and verify your site.

Once verified, submit your sitemap at: fenkellrxpharmacy.com/sitemap.xml
